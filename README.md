## Who can use Organized?

The app is available for all congregation members, including elders, ministerial servants, pioneers, publishers, and even midweek meeting students ‚Äì everyone will see its benefits. However, we kindly suggest discussing its use with your body of elders, as local circumstances and directions may vary.

## What are the device requirements to open CPE?

CPE is a web application, and it is also PWA capable. All devices that have up to date browser, depending on which platform it is running can use this web application.
